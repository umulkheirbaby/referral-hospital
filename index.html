OUR SERVICES

Surgical

GLTRH conducts numerous major and minor surgeries and draws together faculty and staff from several departments (a multi-disciplinary team) to enhance patient care and research..

Medical

The Medical Services Division coordinates and manages Paediatrics, Cardiology, Mental Health, Nutrition, Social Work, Cancer Treatment Centre, Renal, Respiratory Infectious Disease Unit, Endoscopy, Comprehensive Care Centre, Endocrinology/Diabetes Centre and Haemophilia.

Diagnostics

The Diagnostics Services and Health Information Division provides Radiological, Laboratory, Farewell and Health Information services to support clinical excellence.

Pharmaceutical

The Pharmaceutical Services Division coordinates and manages the Clinical Pharmacy, Pharmacovigilance, Poison & Drug Information, Pharmaceutical Products, and Pharmaceutical Production in order to ensure adherence to professional best practices.

Our Oxygen Plant

Garissa County, Kenya â€“ In a significant boost to healthcare infrastructure in northern Kenya, the United States Agency for International Development (USAID) has donated a state-of-the-art oxygen plant to the Garissa Referral Hospital. This facility is the first and only of its kind in the region, providing a critical lifeline to patients in need of oxygen therapy.
The oxygen plant is a vital addition to the hospital's capabilities, particularly in the context of the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. Oxygen therapy is essential for patients with respiratory distress, including those suffering from severe cases of the virus. The plant's capacity will ensure a consistent and reliable supply of oxygen, improving patient outcomes and saving lives.
