Our Vision

A center of excellence in provision of accessible, affordable and socially acceptable quality health care services in the region and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide curative, rehabilitative, and preventive health care services to all.

Core values

Teamwork

We will undertake our work collectively and share our achievements.

Integrity

We will cultivate honesty in our service delivery system.

Gender Sensitivity

We will carry out affirmative action in appointing staff to positions of responsibilities.

Equality

We will provide the best health care services to all, irrespective of race, colour, religion and socio-economic status.
